Reception Services

Accommodations

Temple Inn and Suites are host to very beautiful and afforable wedding receptions for newly-weds. There are many accommodations for food, decorations, and setup for just-married couples and their families attending.

Food Accommodations

There are multiple food accommodations for weddings and receptions services at temples inns and suites. To start, portions can be offered in large amounts, all the way up to 250 guests. There are many kinds of protiens offered, such as: beef, chicken, pork, seafood, and lamb. More can be requested if desired. There are also many sides that can come with the proteins, as well as veggies. Mashed potatos, salads, vegetable mixes: you name it! The wedding cake may also be accommodated as well, but you must call and notify the catoring services if that is to be done.

Setup

Temple Inn and Suites have multiple gorgeous locations and set-ups for a reception. The main attraction of most weddings held is a large, 80 x 64 ballroom that can seat up to 300 guests with room to spare for a dance floor and any fun acitivites desired. These ballrooms are also accompanied by large windows that allow the guests to see across a beautiful landscape. If you wish for something smaller, we have plenty of medium-sized rooms or, possibly, a large outdoor set up with a canopy, tables, and room for activites.
