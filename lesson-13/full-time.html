Rooms for Full-Time Missionaries

Twin Room

The Twin Room containers two single-person beds in one bedroom. The room comes with a TV with plenty of entertaining channels to watch, free Hotel Wi-Fi, a private bathroom, microwave, mini-fridge, and work desk. The bathroom comes with a small bottle of shampoo, conditioner, two bars of soap, a counter-top sink, shower, and towels. Residents may also call for room service if desired and extra supplies if needed.

Double Queen Room

The Double Queen Room containers two queen-sized beds in one bedroom. As with the twin room, there is a TV, free Hotel Wi-Fi, a private bathroom, microwave, mini-fridge, bookshelf, and work desk. The bathroom comes with a medium bottle of shampoo, conditioner, two bars of soap, a countertop sink, shower + bath combination, and plenty of towels. There is also room service if desired and extra supplies if needed.

Double King Room

The Double King Room containers two king-sized beds in one bedroom with a divider between the two for privacy. As with both rooms, there is a TV, free Hotel Wi-Fi, two large private bathrooms, microwave, kettle, mini-fridge, study area, and work desk, all in a large area. The bathroom comes with a large bottle of shampoo, conditioner, two bars of soap, hand lotions, a counter-top sink, shower + bath combination, and towels. There is also room service if desired and extra supplies if needed.
