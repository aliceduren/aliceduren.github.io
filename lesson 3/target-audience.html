Target Audience

This target audience for this app are teachers and parents that live in Idaho (or the select cites that weather is reported in). Generally college graudates in their mid 30's-40's making 50-60k a year. They're main motivation is to prepare for the day and check the future weather conditions for their work/ how their kids should dress or what they should do during the day.
